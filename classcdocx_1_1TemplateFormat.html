Formatting specification for template values

Chainable builder for text formatting. Unspecified fields are ignored unless FormatPolicy::Override is set.

Usage:
auto fmt = cdocx::TemplateFormat()
.bold()
.italic()
.size(14)
.font("SimSun")
.color("FF0000")
.alignment("center");
