Control characters often encountered in documents

Provides constants for special characters used in Word documents, such as paragraph breaks, page breaks, tab characters, etc.

Usage Example:
builder.write("Line 1" + cdocx::ControlChar::paragraph_break());
builder.write("Column A" + cdocx::ControlChar::tab() + "Column B");
